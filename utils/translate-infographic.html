CORE PRINCIPLES FOR SUCCESS
🤝
AI Assists, Humans Decide
Always keep humans at the center to make the final strategic decisions. AI is a force-multiplier, not the decision-maker.
🚢
"Ship Small, Ship Often" Strategy
Use Feature Flags to release features incrementally (5% → 100%) to minimize risk and enable fast rollback.
🧰
Optimize Your Toolset (Avoid Tool Sprawl)
Limit to a maximum of 2 AI tools per phase to avoid waste and keep the workflow focused and efficient.
